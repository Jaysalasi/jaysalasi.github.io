WORKS

NIGERIAN DREAM

The beauty of Nigeria isn't just the geographical location, with the vast arable lands and natural resources but also in diversity of her people. With more than 300 languages and over 180 million people who are resilient and never give up, it brings us to the reminder that to whom much is given much is expected. The imperative and urgent need for good governance can't not be over emphasized, this requires the fight against many things that oppress us as a people. We realise that we can't go where we don't want to go, we can't be one when what united us is merely a geographical location or religion. The bond of the people must be in our collective vision that we will call the Nigeria dream. The Nigeria Dream is a project that will allows citizens both home and abroad to participate in creating and writing the vision of Nigeria. We believe we can and that means we must..
